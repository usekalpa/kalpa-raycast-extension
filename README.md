# Kalpa for Raycast

Capture, organize, and rediscover web content directly from Raycast with AI-powered tagging and summarization.

## Overview

Kalpa for Raycast brings your personal knowledge universe into your command palette. Quickly save any webpage, article, or video with automatic AI tagging and summarization—all without leaving Raycast.

### Key Features

- **One-command saving** – Save any webpage instantly to Kalpa
- **AI-powered tagging** – Automatic categorization and topic extraction
- **AI summaries** – Get quick overviews of saved content
- **Smart organization** – Save to themed universes or your inbox
- **Instant search** – Find saved content by title, tags, or summary
- **Seamless sync** – Syncs with your Kalpa account across devices

## Requirements

- [Raycast](https://raycast.com) (free or Pro)
- [Kalpa account](https://usekalpa.com) (free or Pro)
- macOS 10.15+ (for Raycast compatibility)

## Installation

1. Open Raycast
2. Search for "Kalpa"
3. Click "Install Extension"
4. Sign in with your Kalpa account on first use

## Usage

### Save a page

1. Open Raycast (`Cmd+K` by default)
2. Type "Kalpa: Save Page"
3. Optionally select a universe to organize your save
4. Kalpa fetches content, generates tags and a summary automatically

### View saved content

1. Open Raycast
2. Type "Kalpa: My Saves"
3. Browse or search your saved knowledge

### Search across your knowledge

1. Open Raycast
2. Type "Kalpa: Search"
3. Query by title, tags, or content keywords

## Preferences

Customize the extension in Raycast → Extensions → Kalpa:

- **Default Universe** – Choose where new saves go if not specified
- **Auto-sign in** – Remember your Kalpa account (recommended)
- **Show summaries** – Toggle AI summaries in quick view
